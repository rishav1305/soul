## Why Networking Is The Strategy

- 60-80% of senior roles are never posted
- 85% of positions filled through referrals/networking
- Referred candidates 15x more likely to be hired
- Success rate: 33-80% (networking) vs 4-10% (job boards)

## Core Principle

**Lead with value, discover intent.** Never open with what you want. The same connection could lead to a job, freelance project, consulting call, or team contract.

## Contact Types & Intent

| Contact Type | Intent | Min Exchanges Before Ask |
|---|---|---|
| Hiring Manager (AI lab) | Job | 7+ (READY) |
| Founder / CTO | Freelance/Contract | 2-3 (ACTIVE) |
| Peer Engineer | Referral | 4-5 (WARM) |
| Researcher | Relationship | No direct ask |
| VC / Investor | Intel | Never ask |
| Community Leader | Visibility | Never ask |
| Dev Advocate | Amplification | Never ask |

## Channel Strategy

| Channel | Volume/week | Your Time |
|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn** (outbound) | 15-20 connects + 10-15 comments | 20 min |
| **X/Twitter** (engagement) | 10-15 replies | 10 min |
| **Email** (freelance outreach) | 3-5 emails | 5 min |
| **Communities** (observe-first) | Your pace | When comfortable |

## Warmth Scoring

| Level | Interactions | Ask Eligibility |
|---|---|---|
| NEW | 0-1 | No ask |
| ACTIVE | 2-3 | Founder/CTO ask OK |
| WARM | 4-6 | Peer ask OK |
| READY | 7+ | Dream company ask OK |
| DORMANT | 30+ days inactive | Re-engage first |

## AI Pipeline (3 Gates)

- **Gate N1** (Tue/Thu, 20 min): Review outreach drafts
- **Gate N2** (Fri, 10 min): Weekly networking brief
- **Gate N3** (as needed): Conversion decisions

**40-60 touchpoints/week, ~50 min of your time.**
