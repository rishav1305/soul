## Scope

Team/company contract work: your TEAM does the work. Assemble per-project from network + freelancer pool. No bench, zero overhead until signed.

## Three Engagement Models

| Model | Billing | Your Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Staff Augmentation** | Per-person monthly | Resource provider + QA |
| **Project Delivery** | Milestone / fixed-price | Tech lead + PM |
| **Retainer / Dedicated Team** | Monthly retainer | Fractional CTO / AI lead |

## 3-Phase Discovery

### Phase 1: Freelance Upsell (Month 1-3)
- Freelance client needs more scope → propose your team
- AI detects upsell triggers (scope expansion, client hiring, growing project)
- Target: convert 1 in 5 freelance clients
- **₹1-5L/month** deals

### Phase 2: Targeted Outreach (Month 3-6)
- TheirStack: companies hiring 3+ AI roles = "need a team"
- AI generates company-specific pitch (CompanyPitch tool)
- 3-5 outreach sequences/week
- **₹5-15L/month** deals

### Phase 3: B2B Inbound (Month 6+)
- Clutch/GoodFirms profiles with case studies
- Content-driven inbound + referrals
- **₹15L+/month** deals

## AI Pipeline (3 Gates)

- **Gate C1** (weekly): Review opportunities — pursue, edit pitch, skip
- **Gate C2** (per deal): SOW/terms decision — accept, counter, walk away
- **Gate C3** (per completion): Testimonial, case study, upsell, referral

## Margin Model

Client pays ₹1-4L/mo per team member. You pay team ₹40K-2L/mo. 40-60% margin + your own rate at ₹4-6L/mo.

## Revenue Trajectory

| Period | Monthly | Combined w/ Freelance |
|---|---|---|
| Month 1-3 | ₹1-3L | ₹1.5-4.5L |
| Month 3-6 | ₹3-8L | ₹4.5-13L |
| Month 6+ | ₹8-15L | ₹13-20L |
| Month 12+ | ₹15-30L | ₹20-35L |

## Time: ~3-4 hrs/week (Phase 1) → ~7-10 hrs/week (Phase 3, mostly management)
