## Month 2-3 Focus

- Freelance: first reviews accumulating → raise rates
- Networking: contacts moving to WARM/READY → conversion conversations
- Consulting: first expert network acceptances → first calls
- Contracts: identify first upsell opportunity from freelance
- Content: engagement patterns clear → pillar rebalancing
- Profile: first case study from freelance work → portfolio update
- GitHub: strategic repo v1 complete

## Month 4-6 Focus

- Freelance: shift to vetted networks + direct clients
- Contracts: first team contract signed
- Consulting: first advisory retainer
- Content: inbound leads growing → content-to-lead tracking meaningful
- Revenue target: ₹5-13L/month across all streams
