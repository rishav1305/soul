Pattern: Each product exports a TopBarActions component

The AppLayout renders a shared top bar shell with product icon + name on the left. Each product page exports a TopBarActions component that fills the right side. Chat's includes sessions toggle. Tasks includes view switcher. Products without custom actions get just the icon + name.
